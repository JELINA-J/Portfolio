# 🌐 Personal Portfolio

Welcome to my personal portfolio website! This portfolio showcases my skills, projects, education, certifications, and achievements as a Computer Science and Engineering student passionate about Full-Stack Development, Machine Learning, and AI.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The portfolio is designed to provide recruiters, employers, and fellow developers with a comprehensive overview of my technical skills, academic background, and project experience through a clean, responsive, and user-friendly interface.

Visitors can:

* 👩‍💻 Learn more about me
* 🎓 View my education and certifications
* 💼 Explore my technical skills
* 🚀 Browse featured projects
* 📄 Download my resume
* 📬 Contact me through the contact section
* 📱 Access the portfolio seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices
